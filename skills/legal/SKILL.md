## Common Scenarios

### OÜ Setup
```
Checklist:
☐ Name availability check (ariregister)
☐ Notary appointment (asutamisleping)
☐ Share capital: min 0.01 EUR (обычно 2500 EUR)
☐ Management board appointment
☐ Bank account opening
☐ Tax registration (EMTA)
☐ E-residency card (if needed)

Timeline: 1-3 дня
Cost: ~200-400 EUR (notary + fees)
```

### Service Contract Essentials
```
Обязательные пункты:
1. Isikud (Стороны)
2. Teenus (Предмет договора)
3. Hind (Цена, VAT)
4. Tähtajad (Сроки)
5. Intellektuaalomand (IP rights)
6. Konfidentsiaalsus (NDA)
7. Lõpetamine (Расторжение)
8. Kohaldatav õigus (Применимое право: Eesti)
9. Vaidluste lahendamine (Арбитраж)

Язык: ET (официальный) + RU (перевод)
```

### GDPR Compliance
```
Must-have:
☐ Privacy Policy (isikuandmete töötlemise põhimõtted)
☐ Cookie consent
☐ Data processing agreements (for processors)
☐ Right to erasure process
☐ Data breach notification plan (<72h)

For Studiokook:
- Customer contact forms
- Order processing
- Newsletter (voluntary consent)
```
