# Business Planning Rubric

## Dimensions

### 1. Market analysis (Анализ рынка)
- 1: No market data
- 2: Generic industry overview
- 3: Estonian market size + trends
- 4: Segmented market data with growth projections and positioning
- 5: TAM/SAM/SOM for Estonian kitchen furniture + adjacent markets analysis

### 2. Financial model (Финансовая модель)
- 1: No numbers
- 2: Revenue targets without backing
- 3: Cost structure + revenue model
- 4: Unit economics + break-even analysis + scenarios (pessimistic/realistic/optimistic)
- 5: Full P&L projection with sensitivity analysis and funding requirements

### 3. Execution plan (План исполнения)
- 1: Wishlist of features/activities
- 2: Timeline without dependencies
- 3: Phased rollout with milestones
- 4: OKR-driven plan with resource allocation and risk register
- 5: Sprint-level plan with decision gates, pivot criteria, and team structure

### 4. Competitive positioning (Конкурентное позиционирование)
- 1: "No competitors" or no mention
- 2: Names competitors
- 3: Feature comparison matrix
- 4: Competitive moats + differentiation strategy with evidence
- 5: Competitive dynamics analysis with response scenarios and defensive strategy

### 5. Risk assessment (Оценка рисков)
- 1: No risks mentioned
- 2: Lists obvious risks
- 3: Risk matrix (probability × impact)
- 4: Mitigation strategies per risk + early warning indicators
- 5: Pre-mortem analysis + contingency plans + portfolio risk view
